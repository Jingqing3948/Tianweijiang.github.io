Intro

What I am all about.

I'm Jiang Tianwei, a soon-to-be graduate from the Internet of Things Engineering program at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. My journey has been a blend of software and hardware, with hands-on experience in cloud computing, big data, and microprocessor design. I've honed my skills through internships and personal projects, always with an eye towards innovation. Beyond tech, I'm passionate about track and field, photography, and video editing, balancing my life with creativity and physical activity. In essence, I'm a tech enthusiast and a lover of life, always eager to explore and innovate.
